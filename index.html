VIT Life - College Lifestyle & Management Portal for VIT Bhopal

VIT Life is the premier centralized platform designed specifically for students of VIT Bhopal. It integrates academic management, campus lifestyle coordination, and student growth tools into a single responsive application.

Key Features

  • Dashboard: Access current updates, check database connectivity, and stay informed on daily university announcements.
  • Campus Life & Events: Discover campus events, technical workshops, cultural fests, and student club recruitment schedules. Manage event registration and poster details.
  • Academic Timetable: Keep track of your daily classes, schedule reminders, and synchronize your VIT Bhopal timetable directly to your device.
  • Opportunities Hub: Explore career opportunities, tech internships, open-source programs, and student resources customized for engineering growth.
  • VIT Bhopal Guide: Navigational information on hostels, academic regulations, campus facilities, and local utilities.
  • Interactive Skill Roadmap: A progressive roadmap tracing linear algebra, numerical methods, software engineering, and machine learning foundations.
